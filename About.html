About

    OsinaChi Franklin Ehiemere is a Minister of the Lord Jesus Christ committed with preaching the Word of Truth with signs following by the Will of God.

    He has served in various capacities as a Coordinator of Foursquare Students' Fellowship, Nnammdi Azikwe University Chapter. He also served during His National Service as a Zonal Coordinator of NCCF, Ankpa, Kogi State. He is single and resides in Lagos, where he works as a software engineer in BT Technologies Limited is waiting on the Lord's commissioning.
